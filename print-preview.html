Notes on Completing the Template

Strategic Focus

This should be any of the seven (7) Strategic Focus /pillars in the Strategic Plan: They are: People, Processes, Planet, Relevance, Impact, Sustainability and Continuous Improvement.

Strategic Objective No

This is the objective number under the Strategic Focus you have quoted in the first column.

Strategic Initiatives

This is the specific strategic initiative in the Strategic Plan that is assigned to you under the Strategic Focus you have quoted in the first column.

Commencement Date

This should be the commencement date stated in the Strategic Plan for a Strategic Initiative.

Completion Date

This should be the commencement date stated in the Strategic Plan for a Strategic Initiative.

Update / Progress Made in the Quarter

This is to contain a report on that specified Strategic Initiative you are providing to Management and Council.

Status

You are to state one of these statuses for each Strategic Initiatives for the reporting quarter.

Each row in the table should be used for only one Strategic Initiative assigned to you (and /or Directorate/Centre/Faculty/Department/etc). The Strategic Initiatives should also follow the same pattern. .

The listing of the Strategic Focus in the table should follow the arrangement in the Strategic Plan, with People being first and Continuous Improvement being last.
